Welcome Stranger!

Welcome to Fishdom Club! Where a passion for (virtual) aquatic life brings us together! At Fishdom, we invite you to dive into a vibrant community where we not only play the immersive Fishdom game but also foster a space to mingle and discuss our shared love for all things aquatic.

Join us as we embark on exciting virtual adventures, strategizing and exploring the depths of the Fishdom game while enjoying casual hangouts where we exchange fascinating fish tales. Whether you're a seasoned aquarist or just beginning to explore the world of aquatic wonders, Fishdom Club is the perfect place to engage, learn, and have fun together. Come, be a part of our welcoming community where we not only play but also educate one another about the marvels of new fish species that the game and real life have to offer. Discover the joy of aquatic camaraderie -- join Fishdom Club today!

P.S. We actually go fishing in real life! In fact, we hit the waters about once a month for some real-life fishing fun! Picture us casting lines, soaking up the sun, and swapping fishing stories while we hook the big ones. Come along and reel in the good times with us!

Weekly Schedule

  • Every Saturday from 1pm-3pm @ the couch on 3rd Floor of WALC
  • Every Third Sunday from 4pm-6pm @ Rachel's House
  • Whenever we feel like it (~once a month) to go fishing (except in December and January)
